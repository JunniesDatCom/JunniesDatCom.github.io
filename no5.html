How to make "Lasagna"

Things to prepare : Meat Sauce [onion/garlic/celery/beef/canned tomato/tomato paste/red wine/seasonings(stock cubes/bay leaves/oregano/worcestershire sauce)]
White sauce [butter/flour/milk/cheese]
Etcetera [fresh lasagna sheets/cheese/tools to cook/place to cook]

Meat Sauce

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook beef and pork in the hot skillet until crumbly and no longer pink(5~7 min). Remove from the heat and set aside.
  2. Warm olive oil in another large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic in hot oil until onions are tender, about 5 minutes. Pour in 1/2 cup wine and mix well.
  3. Add vegetable sthat you prepared and thyme to onion mixture. Pour in another 1/2 cup wine, cook and stir until mushrooms are tender(5 min).
  4. Add browned meat, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to mushroom mixture. Reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Pour in remaining 2 cups wine. Continue simmering for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.

White Sauce

  1. Whisk flour into melted butter until the roux is thick and golden
  2. Slowly whisk in the milk. Cook and stir until the thickened.

layering up

  1. Smear a bit of sauce on the base.
  2. Top with meat sauce, bit of white sauce.
  3. Repeat.
  4. When your layering is done, cover with lasagna sheets and pour over remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Pop it in the oven.
